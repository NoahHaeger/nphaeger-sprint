Key Features

StudyPulse focuses on both time and quality so students can make better academic decisions.

Log Study Sessions

Record study start and end times so students can track how long they spend preparing for each class.

🎯

Project concept

About StudyPulse

This project was created as a BAIS 3300 product concept for students who want more than a timer. StudyPulse is built around the idea that time studied and focus level should both be measured.

The goal is to help students study smarter, reflect on their habits, and improve performance over time.
